### Why It Matters

Decentralization is a core metric of network health. Concentration of nodes in a single geography or hosting provider creates single points of failure:

| Risk | Impact |
|------|--------|
| Data center outage | Loss of significant network capacity |
| Regional regulation | Forced shutdown of concentrated nodes |
| Provider policy change | Mass node disconnection |
| Natural disaster | Geographic cluster goes offline |
